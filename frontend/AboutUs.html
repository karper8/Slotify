About Us

We are on a mission to redefine warehouse operations with our intelligent slotting optimization platform. By harnessing the power of predictive analytics, machine learning, and real-time warehouse insights, we automate and simplify the process of assigning the most efficient storage locations for your SKUs — improving speed, accuracy, and efficiency across your operations.

What We Do

Our platform empowers warehouses to maximize space utilization, enhance picking speed, and streamline workflows. By analyzing demand patterns, SKU volumes, co-picking behavior, and real-time activity data, we deliver data-driven slotting recommendations that adapt as your operations evolve.

Key Features

  • ABC & FSN Classification: Prioritize high-frequency SKUs near pick zones
  • Heatmap Visualization: Identify congested or underutilized areas
  • Travel Time Minimization: Optimize picker routes across the warehouse
  • Smart Space Allocation: Use volume and co-location data for efficient slotting
  • Flexible Integration: Works across various warehouse layouts and setups

Our Vision

We believe in a future where warehouses run smarter, not harder. By blending automation, intelligence, and adaptability, our goal is to transform warehouses of every scale into high-performance, cost-efficient, and future-ready facilities.
